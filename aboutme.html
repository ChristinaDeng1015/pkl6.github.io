The Life of Peter

Welcome to my little corner on zhe web, I hope you enjoy your stay, feel free to migrate through my portfolio below, or scroll down if you're feeling a little adventurous and wish to read more about the ups and downs of my life.

In the early months of 2012 as I was nearing the end of my business degree, I came across something that changed my life forever. It was 2am in the morning as I sat in my room, reading on articles on how responsive web comics will soon take over and old traditional style of comics will soon be a thing of the past. As a big fan of comics, I’ve always found it hard to read comics while using my smartphone at that time, an iPhone 4 and the idea that something so radical will soon come and bring changes to how any type of information will be consumed by users simply fascinated me to no end. But what’s amazed me even more was the fact that I was still awake reading about responsive design at 2am in the morning. In the two years that follow, I began my transition from working in the digital marketing field to a graphic designer and ultimately a UX/UI designer. I had the privilege of working with companies like Cymax and Evilnut which allowed me to excerise my digital marketing skills but at the same time developed my skills as a user interface designer. Five years ago, I could never exert the same amount of effort towards the business courses I am taking or spend hours reading on articles about the market. It was then that I realized that passion is extremely important for me if I ever want to excel towards something I truly love and be great at it.
