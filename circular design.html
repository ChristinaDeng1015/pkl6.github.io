Circular Design

A design study on the current shifting landscape of the design field. In the past few years we are begining to see a shift from product focus to company focus and now to economic focus. This shift in design allows us to expand on who we are designing for as well as ultilizing new tevhnologies to creat a future that is only limited by our own imagination. Circular Design has been the fundamental building block of companies such as Tesla and Ecovative and Ideo has taken the initiative to set the standards for the next design guide.
